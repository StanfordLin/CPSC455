Hello, I'm Stanford, a 5th year student. The project uses a simple field input form to add additional recipes. The ingredients and steps can be adjusted to the text area and are delimited by a semi-colon for any additional ingredients/steps. It adds additional recipes using "insertAdjacentHTML" via javascript.


For the responsiveness of the website, fontawesome icons such as the hamburger and "X" were used for the navigation bar drawer when the window is less than 678px.
